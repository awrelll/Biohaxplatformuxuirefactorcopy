## üî¨ TrueAge Testing (TruDiagnostic)

### What It Measures

The TrueAge test is a cutting-edge epigenetic assessment that determines your biological age by analyzing **over 1 million methylation sites** on your DNA. Unlike chronological age, biological age reveals your body's true state of aging.

### Key Metrics

- **Biological Age vs Chronological Age** - Are you aging faster or slower than your years?
- **Pace of Aging** - Your current rate of aging progression
- **System-Specific Ages:**
  - Brain Age
  - Heart Age
  - Liver Age
  - Immune Age
  - Hormone Age
  - Inflammatory Age
- **Telomere Length** - Cellular aging markers
- **Early Dysfunction Signals** - Detection years before symptoms appear

### How BioHax Uses TrueAge Data

2. **Dual-Engine AI Analysis** - OpenBioLLM + Google Gemini cross-verify your methylation patterns
3. **Cross-Reference with Current Biomarkers** - AI correlates epigenetic markers with blood work, wearables, and lifestyle data
4. **Precision Protocol Generation** - Get targeted interventions based on your specific aging patterns
5. **Longitudinal Tracking** - Re-test every 6-12 months to track biological age reversal

### Typical Use Cases

- **Accelerated Immune Aging** ‚Üí Protocol: Targeted supplements (NAD+, resveratrol), stress management, sleep optimization
- **Elevated Inflammatory Age** ‚Üí Protocol: Anti-inflammatory diet, gut healing, elimination of inflammatory triggers

## ü¶† Geneva GI Effects Test

### What It Measures

The Geneva GI Effects Comprehensive Stool Profile uses combined methodologies (PCR, stool cultures, microscopy) to provide the most advanced gut health diagnostic available.

### Five Key Biomarker Areas

1. **Maldigestion** - Enzyme function, fat absorption, protein breakdown
2. **Inflammation** - Gut barrier integrity, immune activation
3. **Dysbiosis** - Microbial balance, beneficial vs pathogenic bacteria
4. **Metabolite Imbalance** - SCFA production, pH levels
5. **Infection** - Pathogen detection with subtyping, parasites, H. pylori

### Advanced Features

- **Methanogens & H2S Producers** - Quantification of gas-producing bacteria
- **Pathogen Subtyping** - Specific strain identification
- **Antibiotic Resistance Markers** - If pathogens detected
- **Fungal Overgrowth Detection** - Candida and other yeasts

### How BioHax Uses GI Effects Data

1. **Automatic Parsing** - Upload your Geneva Diagnostics PDF results
2. **Biomarker Tracking** - All 5 areas become trackable metrics in your dashboard
3. **Dual-Engine AI Correlation** - Links gut health to systemic inflammation, mood, energy, immune function
4. **Root Cause Identification** - AI identifies hidden drivers of:
   - IBS and digestive issues
   - Skin problems (acne, eczema, psoriasis)
   - Mood disorders (anxiety, depression, brain fog)
   - Autoimmune-like symptoms
   - Chronic fatigue
5. **Actionable Protocols** - Personalized supplement stacks, dietary adjustments, probiotic recommendations

### Typical Use Cases

- **SIBO (Methanogens Detected)** ‚Üí Protocol: Low-FODMAP diet, antimicrobial herbs, prokinetics
- **Gut Inflammation + Low SCFA** ‚Üí Protocol: Resistant starch, butyrate supplementation, anti-inflammatory diet
- **Pathogen Overgrowth** ‚Üí Protocol: Targeted antimicrobials, biofilm disruptors, gut barrier repair
